Homemade Pizza

A classic, crispy, and delicious pizza that you can make right in your own kitchen with just a few simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 Â½ cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of dry yeast
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix the flour and olive oil into the water to form a dough.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for 10 minutes.
  4. Let the dough rise in a warm place for an hour before baking.
